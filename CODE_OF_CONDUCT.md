# Code of Conduct

**Research Use Only. Not for clinical use.**

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include:
- demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- focusing on what is best for the community and users

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:
- harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- publishing others’ private information
- making unsubstantiated clinical claims or encouraging unsafe medical use

## Safety and Medical-Adjacent Considerations

Because this project is medical-adjacent:
- Do not pressure others to self-experiment.
- Do not claim the software treats or cures disease.
- Do not post personal health information in issues or discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behaviour they deem inappropriate.
